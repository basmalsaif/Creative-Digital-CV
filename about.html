About the initiative

Reimagining academic resumes for the digital era.

Creative Digital CV bridges the gap between traditional curriculum vitae formats and the interactive expectations of modern recruiters. The project blends UX-driven storytelling with robust system architecture to deliver a frictionless experience for both creators and reviewers.

Impact Snapshot

Designed for capstone presentations and industry showcases, the solution elevates how IT seniors present their professional journeys.

Project objectives

Each objective aligns with academic assessment criteria while ensuring usability, accessibility, and technological scalability.

Digital storytelling excellence

Craft a guided CV experience with modular storytelling blocks that adapt to diverse career paths and portfolios.

Ease of content management

Enable quick edits, template switching, and export options without compromising layout fidelity.

Integration-ready architecture

Design a scalable front-end foundation that can connect to APIs, analytics, and authentication layers.

Success metrics
  • 90%+ positive feedback from peer reviews.
  • Responsive layouts validated across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
  • Seamless transition from static prototype to deployable MVP.
