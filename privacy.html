Privacy Notice

This Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Creative Digital CV.

1. Introduction

We prioritize privacy and only collect what we need to provide, secure, and improve the service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 You provide directly

  • Basic account details you choose to share (e.g., name, email).
  • Content you enter into the CV builder (profile, experience, skills, etc.).
  • Messages you send via support or contact forms.

2.2 Automatically

Limited technical information may be collected to keep the service reliable and secure (for example, device and browser type, general usage events, and diagnostics). We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories unless you provide them.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate product features you request.
  • Maintain reliability, prevent abuse, and enhance user experience.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.

4. Storage & Technologies

To deliver a fast and resilient experience, the service may use client-side storage and similar technologies. These are used only to support core functionality (for example, remembering your progress and preferences) and are not used to track you across other sites.

You can clear saved data using your browser settings. Doing so may remove drafts or preferences you have saved for this site.

Where sign-in with external providers is offered, the provider handles authentication and may share basic profile information necessary to enable your account in line with their policies.

5. Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; please avoid placing secrets or passwords in free-text fields.

6. Retention

  • Information stored on your device remains until you remove it or your browser clears it.
  • Support and account information may be retained as long as needed to operate the service and meet legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, object to, or receive a copy of certain information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may request information to verify your identity.
